First Steps

1. Click and drag the piano block onto the workspace.

2. Drag in a "green block". Connect these together.

3. Click on the dark blue plus button and click an option.

4. A note will appear. Move this by pressing the arrows shown when your mouse on a note.

5. Click the play button. You should hear the note playing!
