Codetta

Supporting children in music composition!

By Corey Ford

What is Codetta?

Codetta is an app making composing music easy for anyone! Developed through collaborations with teachers in training and practising educators, Codetta adopts established ideas from programming to support beginners with creating music. By simply snapping colourful puzzle-like blocks together, any novice can learn how to write expressive music.
