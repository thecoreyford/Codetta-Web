Clefs

The clef-blocks will change the pitch values for subsequent notes, until interrupted by another clef block.

The treble clef means that the notes on the lines are best for high sounding instruments.

The bass clef means that the notes on the lines are best for low sounding instruments... like the cello!

If you forget what the pitches are called, you can look at the info bar at the bottom of Codetta.

By default, the treble clef is selected.
