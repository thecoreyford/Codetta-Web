Tempo Changer

The tempo-changer block will increment (or decrement) the beats-per-minute for any blocks connected to the right, until interrupted by another tempo block.

Beats-per-minute (or BPM) is related to the tempo of the piece.

When used alongside the repeat blocks , accelerandos and ritardandos can occour.

This is where the music gets faster and faster (accelerandos), or gradually gets slower (ritardandos).
