Corey Ford

    PhD Researcher in AI, HCI and Music

I am a PhD researcher working within the intersection of Child-Computer Interaction, Music and Artificial Intelligence. My research is centred around designing intelligent agents for Codetta: a block-based digital musical interface that I developed, in order to support childrens' creativity in music composition.

Interests: Human-Computer Interaction, Computer-Supported Education, Intelligent Tutoring Systems, Intelligent User Interfaces, (Educational) Data Mining, Creativity, Machine-Learning, Pedagogy, NIME, C++, audio, music
